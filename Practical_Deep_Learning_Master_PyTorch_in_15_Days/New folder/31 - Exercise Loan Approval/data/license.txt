This dataset is a synthetic version inspired by the original Credit Risk dataset 
on Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/laotse/credit-risk-dataset) and enriched 
with additional variables based on Financial Risk for Loan Approval data. SMOTENC 
was used to simulate new data points to enlarge the instances.
The dataset is structured for both categorical and continuous features.
